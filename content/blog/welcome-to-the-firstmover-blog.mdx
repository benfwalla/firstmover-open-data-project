We're launching the FirstMover blog to share what we're learning from tracking every new rental listing in NYC.

Every month, we process tens of thousands of StreetEasy listings. Now we're turning that data into insights anyone can use — whether you're apartment hunting, a journalist covering the housing market, or just curious about what's happening with NYC rents.

Expect monthly rent reports, neighborhood deep dives, and guides for navigating the NYC rental market backed by real data, not vibes.

Stay tuned.
